# HMFFL — HeMan Fantasy Football League

## The Problem
The league is managed entirely via spreadsheets and email (draft, rosters, lineups, scores, waivers). The rules are unique enough that standard fantasy tools are useless.

## The Team
**Team:** Bojjaes (co-managed)
**League:** HMFFL (HeMan Fantasy Football League)

## Unique Rules
- **Format:** Dynasty league with a salary cap ($100/team, 24 players + 2 IR)
- **Rookie salaries:** $2, $1, or $0 depending on draft position; salary increases $1–4/year based on NFL stats
- **Dropped players:** Once dropped and unclaimed for a few weeks, they're gone from the league permanently. Same for undrafted/unclaimed rookies.
- **Scoring:**
  - Rushing/receiving yards: 0 pts until ~70-yard threshold in a game, then 3 pts; +1 per 10 yards after
  - Touchdowns (rush/rec/pass): 6 pts each
  - Field goals: 3/4/5 pts (high value relative to yardage scoring)
  - Individual defensive players (no team defense)

## Goal
Build tools to make managing the Bojjaes easier — automating/improving what's currently done manually via spreadsheets and email.
